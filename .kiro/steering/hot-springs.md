# Hot Springs in Vintage Story

## Current Functionality

### Heat Mechanics
- Hot springs only heat blocks in **direct contact** with the boiling water
- The heat radius is limited to 1 block from the hot spring source
- Standing near a hot spring raises body temperature if below 37.8°C

### Mob Interactions
- Hot springs can kill drifters and other mobs that enter them
- Can be used as a mob grinder for trapping and killing mobs
- Mobs will walk into hot springs and be killed by the heat

### Building with Hot Springs
- A sealed space (max 7x7x7 blocks) with one open block above provides thermal protection
- Can build structures over hot springs to keep them warmer
- Hot springs are shallow, making corpse retrieval easy

### Generation
- Hot springs are natural world generation features
- Typically found on suevite stones (meteor impact sites) or other specific rock types
- Generate organically during world generation alongside other features

### Hot Spring Structure
- Hot springs contain bacterial mats at different temperatures
- The bacteria themselves represent the heat source (no single "hot spring block")
- Temperature is determined by the specific bacteria block type

### Bacteria Types and Temperatures
Hot springs contain the following bacterial mat types:
- **hotspringbacteriasmooth-55deg**: Low-temperature bacteria (55°C)
- **hotspringbacteriasmooth-65deg**: Medium-temperature bacteria (65°C)
- **hotspringbacteriasmooth-74deg**: High-temperature bacteria (74°C)
- **hotspringbacteria-87deg**: Very high-temperature bacteria (87°C)

Higher temperature bacteria melt surrounding snow faster and affect a larger radius.
